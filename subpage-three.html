Fiat 600 Multipla

Capible of seating an astonishing six people comfortably, this microcar is infact a microvan! The "seicento" Multipla was first introduced in 1956 and were produced until 1969 by the Italian motor company Fiat, manufacturing a total of 129,994 of these liliputian vans. They are the largest of the microcars featured on this website, clocking in at 11.5' long, and 4.75' wide, but this van has a unique advantage of converting into a tiny camper van, as the two rows of bench seating fold down to be completely flat. What more could you ask for?!
  • The 600 Multipla's Specs:
  • Horsepower: 22 hp
  • Years produced: 1956-1969
  • Units produced: 129,994 Multipla's
  • Size: 4.75' wide, 11.5' long
