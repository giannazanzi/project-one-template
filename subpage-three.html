Fiat 600 Multipla _____________________________________________________

Capible of seating an astonishing six people comfortably, this microcar is infact a microvan! The "seicento" Multipla was first introduced in 1956 and were produced until 1969 by the Italian motor company Fiat, manufacturing a total of 129,994 of these liliputian vans.
